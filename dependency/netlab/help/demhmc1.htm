demhmc1

Purpose

Demonstrate Hybrid Monte Carlo sampling on mixture of two Gaussians.

Synopsis 

demhmc1

Description

The problem consists of generating data from a mixture of two Gaussians in two dimensions using a hybrid Monte Carlo algorithm with persistence. A mixture model is then fitted to the sample to compare it with the true underlying generator.
